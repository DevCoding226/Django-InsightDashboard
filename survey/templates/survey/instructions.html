Otsuka MSL Insights

MSL Insights initiatives train MSLs to have deeper and more insightful discussions with HCPs in the field. This is a collaborative effort between Regional and the Affiliates through prioritising a number of Areas of Interest formulated as appropriate questions for MSL training purposes.

All MSLs must understand the MSL Insights Briefing Document and complete the MSL Insights Launch Briefing in their countries prior to commencing any field activities for this initiative.

User Instructions

  • MSL insights is non-promotional and the Areas of Interest when discussed will be in a non-promotional manner.
  • Discuss the Areas of Interest with HCPs only where appropriate in a natural scientific dialogue.
  • Do not schedule additional HCP meetings specifically for the purpose of gathering MSL Insights for the Areas of Interest.
  • No payment is made to HCP or other parties for MSL Insights initiatives.
  • This Web-app is an internal tool and should not be used in the presence of HCPs and not shown to HCPs.
  • Do not collect any identifiable personal or private data of any kind (i.e. no names of HCPs, patients or hospitals).
  • Do not input any information relating to Adverse events in web-app.
  • Comply with PV and compliance policies and report any Adverse Event uncovered in HCP discussion, including but not limited to off-label use, lack of efficacy, missed dose, unexpected benefit, pregnancy, drug misuse, and overdose/under-dose without an AE.
